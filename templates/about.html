About The Bot

Our Mission

The Ethiopian University Dating Bot is designed to help university students across Ethiopia connect with potential partners in a safe, respectful, and engaging environment. Our platform focuses on creating meaningful connections based on shared interests, universities, and relationship goals.

Features

  • Profile creation with university affiliation
  • Target universities selection for matching
  • Swipe-style matching interface
  • Anonymous chat with matches
  • Secret Crush feature for expressing interest
  • Safety features including blocking and reporting

How It Works

  1. Register with the bot on Telegram and create your profile
  2. Browse potential matches from your selected universities
  3. Like profiles that interest you
  4. When you both like each other, it's a match!
  5. Chat anonymously with your matches
  6. Use the Secret Crush feature to let someone special know you're interested
